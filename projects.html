vellu.me

vellu.me was born because of a personal pain point - the lack of a simple, mostly-text platform to write stuff down. 10+ years ago i was a massive proponent of evernote, but over the years the product has gotten bloated and has lost focus on what it was originally created to be - a simple note-taking platform, but with support for a LOT of deep background features. so i made vellu.me.

as of right now, vellu.me is a web app with a sideloadable android apk. one day i might actually get it up on the play & app store, but for now that works for me. simple, reliable, and secure. supports text notes, voice notes with integrated AI transcription, folders, tags, file attachements, full-text serach, and checkable tasks. live at vellu.me. built with next.js, supabase, vercel, and stripe.

fieldpass

i created fieldpass for a community-driven vanlife event series that i run. i got tired of cookie-cutter ticketing systems that lacked features that i wanted, and forced me and the event attendees to use a workflow that was outdated and overall sub-par.

fieldpass is a ticketing and event management platform aimed at camping festivals, outdoor gatherings, and anyone running an event that needs more than a google form but less than a six-figure enterprise contract. supports flexible ticket types with independent pricing, capacity limits, and early-bird tiers, interactive venue maps for picking camping zones pre-event, a real-time analytics dashboard for revenue and check-ins, automated branded confirmation emails with QR tickets, mobile QR check-in, team and staff invites, and integrated merch. live at fieldpass.app. built with next.js, supabase, vercel, and stripe.
