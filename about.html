About

I'm Lance. I build software and run events, mostly out of a van.

On the software side, I work on the kind of small, focused tools that let little teams and one-person operations do a lot — ticketing, automation, internal dashboards, the occasional web app. I usually reach for Next.js, Supabase, and whatever else is closest at hand.

On the events side, I run VanFest, a series of vanlife festivals in the US.

The rest of the story is on the vanlife page. Or you can just get in touch.
